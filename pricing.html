Common questions

Can I cancel my Pro plan anytime? +
Yes — you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees, no questions asked.
What counts as a "processed" payment for the 3% fee? +
The 3% fee applies only when a vendor pays their stall fee through the Pitch. platform using our integrated payment processor (powered by Stripe). If you agree payment terms directly with vendors and collect payment outside of Pitch. — via bank transfer, cash, or any other method — no fee applies.
Do I need a credit card to sign up? +
No credit card is required to create a free account — for vendors or organisers. You only need to add payment details if you're upgrading to Vendor Pro, or if you're an organiser wanting to enable Pitch. payment processing for your events.
Is there a limit on event applications? +
No. Both Free and Pro vendors can apply to an unlimited number of events. There's no cap, no credits system, and no hidden limits. Apply to as many markets, festivals, and pop-ups as you like.

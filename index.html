How It Works

Your pitch, simplified.

Whether you're a food vendor looking for your next gig or an organiser building a world-class lineup — Pitch. handles the matchmaking.

For vendors
1
Create your profile
Photos, cuisine tags, certifications — build a profile that wins bookings.
2
Get verified
ABN, food safety cert, public liability. Verified vendors rank higher.
3
Apply & get confirmed
Browse events, one-click apply, and hear back within 48 hours — guaranteed.
For organisers
1
Post your event
Dates, location, stall specs, fees. Live in under 5 minutes.
2
Review applications
Verified vendors apply. View profiles, certs, and past events in one place.
3
Approve your lineup
One-click approve. Vendors are notified instantly. Your roster is ready.
See the full breakdown →

Every vendor is verified.

Organizers trust Pitch because every vendor on the platform has passed our three-point verification process before they can apply to a single event.

🏛️
ABN Verified
Every vendor's Australian Business Number is confirmed with the ATO before their profile goes live — no unregistered operators on Pitch.
🍽️
Food Safety Certified
We check that vendors hold a current food safety supervisor certificate and that their local council registration is up to date.
🛡️
$10M Public Liability Insured
All vendors must provide proof of at least $10 million in public liability insurance — the standard requirement for Australian events.

How It Works

Your pitch, simplified.

For vendors
🏪
Create your profile
Build a vendor profile with photos, cuisine tags, certifications, and your past event history. First impressions matter.
✅
Get verified
Submit your ABN, food safety cert, and public liability insurance. Verified vendors get priority placement in search.
🔍
Apply to events
Browse events by location, date, category, and fee range. One-click applications — no chasing emails or phone calls.
⚡
Get confirmed
Organizers review and confirm within 48 hours. You'll always hear back. No ghosting, no uncertainty.
For organisers
📝
Post your event
List your event with dates, location, booth specifications, fees, and requirements. Takes less than 5 minutes.
📥
Receive applications
Verified vendors apply directly to your event. Review their profiles, ratings, certifications, and past events in one place.
🎯
Approve your lineup
One-click approve or decline. Automatic notifications sent to vendors. Build a diverse, quality food lineup with confidence.
🎪
Event day
Your vendor roster is confirmed, contracts are signed, and everyone knows where to show up. You focus on the event.

Every vendor is verified.

Organizers trust Pitch because every vendor on the platform has passed our three-point verification process before they can apply to a single event.

🏛️
ABN Verified
Every vendor's Australian Business Number is confirmed with the ATO before their profile goes live — no unregistered operators on Pitch.
🍽️
Food Safety Certified
We check that vendors hold a current food safety supervisor certificate and that their local council registration is up to date.
🛡️
$10M Public Liability Insured
All vendors must provide proof of at least $10 million in public liability insurance — the standard requirement for Australian events.

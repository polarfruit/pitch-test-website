What we're looking for

We aim to build a diverse lineup that covers a wide range of cuisines and price points. The following categories are preferred — vendors in these categories will receive priority review.

🌮 Street Food 🍱 Asian Cuisine 🥩 BBQ & Grill 🍦 Desserts 🍕 Pizza ☕ Coffee & Drinks 🌾 Vegan / Plant-based 🥙 Middle Eastern
Requirements
🏛️
ABN required — All vendors must have a current Australian Business Number and be registered for GST.
🍽️
Food Safety Supervisor Certificate — At least one person on your team must hold a current FSS cert. You'll need to upload this on your vendor profile.
🛡️
$10M Public Liability Insurance — Must be current and list "City of Adelaide" as an interested party. Certificate of currency required.
📋
Council food business registration — Your food premises must be registered with your local council. We'll verify this during review.
🚫
No deep frying after 8pm — Per council conditions. Vendors using deep fryers must complete service by 8:00pm or have covered fryers.

Cancellation Policy
Cancellations made more than 14 days before the event date will receive a full refund of the booth fee. Cancellations made 7–14 days before the event will receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 7 days of the event are non-refundable. In the event that the event is cancelled by the organiser, all vendors will receive a full refund within 5 business days.

Vendor Responsibilities
Vendors are responsible for complying with all applicable food safety laws, holding current certificates of currency, and ensuring their stall is staffed for the full duration of the event. Vendors must arrive within the designated setup window and vacate the site by the pack-down deadline. Any vendor who does not show up without prior notice will be suspended from the platform.

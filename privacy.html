1. Information We Collect

When you use the Pitch. Platform, we collect information necessary to provide and improve our services. This includes:

Account data — your full name, email address, mobile phone number, and ABN (for vendors). This is collected at registration and is required to create and verify your account.

Profile data — for vendors: business name, food categories, stall setup details, photos, menu descriptions, trading history, and public liability insurance details. For organisers: event listings, venue details, and organiser contact information.

Usage data — information about how you use the Platform, including pages visited, searches performed, applications submitted, and messages sent. This is collected automatically via server logs and optional analytics tools.

Payment data — stall fee payments on the Platform are processed by Stripe. Pitch. does not store full card numbers or bank account details. We receive transaction metadata (amount, date, status) and a reference token from Stripe. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of payment card data.

Verification data — during phone and email verification, we temporarily store the verification code and your contact detail for up to 30 minutes. This data is deleted once verification is complete or expires.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account management — to create, maintain, and secure your account, and to communicate with you about your account activity
  • Identity verification — to verify your email address and phone number upon signup, and to confirm ABN validity for vendor accounts
  • Platform matching — to match vendors with suitable events based on food category, location, and stall requirements, and to surface relevant vendor profiles to organisers
  • Communications — to send transactional messages (application updates, booking confirmations, payment receipts) and, where you've opted in, platform updates and product news
  • Platform analytics — to understand how the Platform is used in aggregate, identify issues, and improve features. Analytics data is anonymised or pseudonymised where possible
  • Legal compliance — to comply with our obligations under Australian law, including tax reporting and fraud prevention

We do not use your personal information for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects without human review.

3. Who We Share It With

We share personal information only where necessary to operate the Platform or as required by law:

  • Event organisers see vendor profile information (business name, food categories, photos, menu, PLI status) when a vendor applies to their event. Organisers do not see vendor email addresses or phone numbers unless the vendor explicitly messages them
  • Vendors see event details and organiser information posted in public event listings. Vendor applications are only visible to the organiser of that specific event
  • Stripe — payment data is shared with Stripe to process stall fee transactions. Stripe operates under its own privacy policy and is PCI-DSS compliant
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers — we use Australian-hosted cloud infrastructure. Our providers process data on our behalf and are bound by data processing agreements
  • Legal obligations — we may disclose personal information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Pitch. or its users

We do not sell personal data to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for their own marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

All personal data collected through the Pitch. Platform is stored on servers located in Australia. We do not transfer personal data outside of Australia without your consent, except where strictly necessary (e.g., Stripe's global payment infrastructure).

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • Data encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • All data in transit encrypted via TLS 1.2 or higher (HTTPS)
  • Session tokens are short-lived and expire after inactivity
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plaintext passwords
  • Access to production databases is restricted to authorised personnel only, via secure authenticated connections

While we take these measures seriously, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorised access to your account.

5. Cookies

The Pitch. Platform uses cookies and similar technologies to function. We use two categories of cookies:

Essential cookies — required for the Platform to operate. These include session cookies (to keep you logged in), CSRF protection tokens, and preference storage. These cannot be disabled without breaking core Platform functionality.

Analytics cookies (optional) — we use Google Analytics to understand how pages are used in aggregate. These cookies collect anonymised data about page views, session duration, and navigation paths. No personally identifiable information is shared with Google Analytics.

You can opt out of analytics cookies at any time by adjusting your browser settings or by using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. Opting out of analytics cookies does not affect Platform functionality.

6. Your Rights (Australian Privacy Act 1988)

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right of Access

You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. We will respond within 30 days.

Right of Correction

If your personal information is inaccurate, incomplete, or out of date, you can request we correct it.

Right of Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and personal data, subject to our retention obligations (see Section 7).

Right to Complain

If you believe we have mishandled your personal information, you can lodge a complaint with the OAIC.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@pitchmkts.com.au. We may need to verify your identity before processing a request.

7. Retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active and for a period thereafter as required by law or legitimate business purposes.

Specifically:

  • Active account data is retained for the duration of your account
  • Upon account deletion, personal data is retained for 7 years to comply with Australian legal and tax obligations (including the Tax Administration Act 1953 and GST Act)
  • After 7 years, personal data is securely deleted or irreversibly anonymised
  • Transaction records (payments processed through Pitch.) are retained for 7 years from the transaction date
  • Verification codes and temporary session data are deleted within 30 minutes of expiry

If you request deletion of your account, we will deactivate your public profile and remove your data from active systems immediately. Archived copies retained for legal compliance will not be accessible to other users or to our customer-facing team.
